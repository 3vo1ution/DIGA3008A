Women Are Gods Image

3th Moon of 2022

Women are God's image. The love of a mother is rivaled by no other: From the tender acts of care, to the happiness we share. How can this not be compared to the love of God? When the presence of a woman radiates the benevolence He proceeds to show us, time and time again. Bringing new life to experience His wonderful creation, while being masterpieces of this creation themselves. How could one not see a reflection of His kindness through the existence of a woman? When women create, love, teach, raise, care, nurture, protect, and give just as God does. When they themselves are indications of the time, precision, thought, and care the Lord put into this work of art we were gifted and lucky enough to call home. Freedom

1st Moon of 2021

In this bucket containing the crabs that are its people, the idea of freedom seems desirable yet unachievable. They see a fraction of the sky above, taunting them with its vastness, yet they are never able to make it out. Gnawing at each other while they all fight for a taste of the heavens, unable to see the goal they all have in common. The ocean waits for them, while they continue to squabble, unable to see that only together are they able to achieve this goal. For the bucket is too high for one to climb, yet as a unit it is but a small feat.
