About Me

As a Digital Arts student at Wits University, I am passionate about the potential of digital arts to drive social activism and community development. My particular interests lie in virtual reality development, animation, and fine arts, and I am committed to amplifying the voices of Black creatives within these fields. I am eager to contribute my diverse skillset to the forefront of technological innovation and creative expression.
