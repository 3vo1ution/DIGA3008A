Week 4

Concept & Theme

This portfolio website is designed as an interactive exploration of my work, blending traditional Egyptian motifs with a sci-fi aesthetic. Inspired by futuristic depictions of ancient civilizations, such as the technology seen in DUNE, the site will feature glowing symbols, stone textures, and a mysterious tomb-like atmosphere filled with hidden treasures. The UI/UX design will take inspiration from this site, integrating smooth transitions, hover effects, and an immersive storytelling approach.

Purpose & Content

The website serves as both an academic project and a personal portfolio, showcasing a diverse range of artistic works, including:
  • Photography
  • Mixed Media (Video, Photography, Digital Illustration)
  • Web Design
  • Game Design
  • Traditional Art
  • Digital Art

Design & Aesthetic Elements

Color Scheme

Dark, atmospheric tones with gold and stone textures, evoking the ambiance of an ancient yet advanced civilization.

Typography & Symbols

Custom glyphs/icons resembling hieroglyphics will be used for navigation and interaction, maintaining a futuristic yet historical feel.

Animations

The site will feature shifting glyphs, glowing artifacts, and floating dust particles to enhance the mystical atmosphere.

Sound Design

Ambient sound effects, including ancient whispers and sci-fi chimes, will create an immersive experience.

Sound Toggle

Users will have the option to enable/disable sound effects.

Navigation & Interactivity

Fluid, Interconnected Layout

Instead of a traditional menu, the site will use a dynamic exploration-based navigation system where sections transition seamlessly while maintaining distinct categories.

Thematic Hubs

Different types of work will be subtly categorized using thematic visual elements (e.g., relics, glowing glyphs, or interactive pathways).

Interactive Animations

Hover-based reveals, smooth scroll effects, and ambient environmental changes will encourage users to explore naturally without disrupting access to content.

Conclusion

This project will combine artistic presentation with interactive web technologies, resulting in a visually and experientially unique portfolio. The goal is to craft an immersive space where users can explore my work organically, experiencing it as if uncovering artifacts of a lost yet advanced civilization.
