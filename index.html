The heart of the tomb: EVO

As a Digital Arts student at Wits University, I am passionate about the potential of digital arts to drive social activism and community development my work is rooted in curiosity, resistance, and care. I’m especially drawn to virtual reality, animation, and fine arts , not just for their creative potential, but for how they can amplify the voices of Black creatives like myself. I want to tell stories that resonate beyond the screen, stories that feel lived-in and uncovered like ancient relics/texts. This site isn’t just a portfolio; it’s an archive of becoming, an archive of me, of passion, process, and a desire to reshape the future etched into the walls of a creation of my own with what I’ve learned, made, and still imagine.
